A Smart Visualization Tool for Kafka





Kafkometry is a lightweight, open-source visualizer for Kafka. With an emphasis on simplicity, Kafkometry is suited to engineers managing small-to-medium scale projects, or who are learning Kafka and want to fast-track their understanding of Kafka's data flow. Kafkometry is powered by Svelte that aims to visualize data from targeted clusters using Prometheus and Grafana. By using Svelte's in built features, data illustrations will be rendered for users at unbelieveable speeds! Your dashboards will only have the diagrams you want, clearing out all the distracting clutter!
